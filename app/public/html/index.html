IDiD Lab

Welcome to the Individual Differences in Development (IDiD) Lab!


The I-DID Lab investigates individual differences in development, with a focus on creativity development and adolescence. We study differentiation and change processes on a variety of topics related to:

  • Creativity and innovative behaviors in various domains including music, writing, and visual arts
  • Conative dimensions important for creativity, such as personality traits, self-concept, and motivational factors
  • Psychological adaptation and psychosocial risks in adolescence, including externalizing problems and juvenile delinquency

A central goal of this research is to develop creative-thinking training programs for adolescents that positively support identity development and help to prevent maladaptive outcomes.
